Torn Invitation

A partially burned invitation was found inside the study fireplace.

The back of the card contains a handwritten message that reads:

"We settle this tonight."

The rest of the message was destroyed by fire.

Broken Pocket Watch

A cracked pocket watch was discovered in the hallway.

The watch stopped at 9:17 PM.

This may indicate when the struggle occurred.

Muddy Boot Print

A muddy boot print was found near a side entrance corridor.

The soil matches the estate grounds rather than the driveway.

This suggests someone may have entered from outside.

Champagne Glass

A half-full champagne glass was left on a table in the music room.

Lipstick residue was present on the rim.

The glass appears to have been abandoned suddenly.

Burned Ledger Page

A partially burned page from Adrian’s financial records was found in his office.

Remaining entries reference large missing funds.

This suggests a possible financial motive.

Silver Key

A small silver key was recovered from a guest bedroom drawer.

The key opens an old storage cabinet unrelated to the office.

Investigators believe this item is not directly related to the murder.
