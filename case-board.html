Case Overview

Here's what we know so far ...

Victor Langley, the owner of Langley Art Gallery, was found dead last night. He was found at his office by a staff member who wanted to talk to Victor. The gallery was hosting a private event, so the murderer has to be one of the guests or the limited staff who were inside the building. However, the killer was not seen on camera since the camera was taken offline at the time of the murder. Who could it be?
