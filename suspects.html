Suspects

Every guest had a reason to want Adrian Vale gone.

Adrian Vale, the wealthy host of the evening, was found dead shortly after the estate dinner ended. These six people were the last known individuals with access to him before the lights went out. Click each suspect card to inspect their file.

Lena Cross

Personal Assistant

Click to inspect suspect file

Lena Cross

Role: Adrian Vale’s personal assistant for the last three years.

Motive: She had recently learned Adrian planned to fire her after accusing her of leaking private information.

Alibi: Claims she was organizing guest records in the study during the time of the murder.

Suspicious Detail: She had access to nearly every room in the house, including Adrian’s private office.

Notes: Calm under pressure, but several staff members heard her arguing with Adrian earlier that night.

Marcus Reed

Business Partner

Click to inspect suspect file

Marcus Reed

Role: Adrian’s longtime business partner and co-owner of several properties.

Motive: Adrian was preparing to remove Marcus from a major investment deal after discovering missing funds.

Alibi: Says he remained in the dining hall speaking with guests after dessert.

Suspicious Detail: A witness says Marcus left the dining hall for several minutes just before the power outage.

Notes: Appears confident and cooperative, but has a strong financial reason to want Adrian silent.

Naomi Hart

Journalist

Click to inspect suspect file

Naomi Hart

Role: Investigative journalist invited to the estate under unclear circumstances.

Motive: She was reportedly gathering evidence for an exposé that Adrian wanted buried at any cost.

Alibi: Claims she was outside on the terrace taking notes and making a phone call.

Suspicious Detail: She was seen carrying a recorder and a folder of documents connected to Adrian’s past.

Notes: She may have uncovered something dangerous, but it is unclear whether she came to expose Adrian or confront him.

Elliot Pike

Estate Groundskeeper

Click to inspect suspect file

Elliot Pike

Role: Groundskeeper who has worked on the Vale estate for over a decade.

Motive: Adrian had recently threatened to sell part of the property, which would have cost Elliot his job and home.

Alibi: Says he was in the greenhouse fixing a broken fuse box when the murder happened.

Suspicious Detail: His boots were found muddy near a hallway he had no reason to use that evening.

Notes: Knows the estate better than anyone and could move through it unnoticed.

Celeste Monroe

Former Fiancée

Click to inspect suspect file

Celeste Monroe

Role: Adrian’s former fiancée, whose engagement ended publicly and badly.

Motive: She believed Adrian ruined her reputation and stole family assets promised to her during their engagement.

Alibi: States she remained in the music room alone after dinner.

Suspicious Detail: Guests overheard her telling Adrian, “You’ll regret this before the night is over.”

Notes: Her anger is obvious, which may make her look guilty even if someone else used it as a distraction.

Theo Bell

Family Friend

Click to inspect suspect file

Theo Bell

Role: Close family friend who had been staying at the estate for the week.

Motive: Adrian had discovered Theo was secretly using his name to cover gambling debts.

Alibi: Claims he was upstairs in the guest wing when the lights went out.

Suspicious Detail: No one can confirm seeing Theo in the guest wing during the actual time of death.

Notes: Friendly and charming, but his story changes slightly each time he tells it.
