Suspects

Everyone at the gallery had a reason to be angry with Victor Lang.

Victor Lang, the owner of the gallery, was found murdered during the exhibition. These six individuals were all present at the event and each had a possible reason to confront him. Click each suspect card to inspect their file.

Daniel Brooks

Featured Artist

Click to inspect suspect file

Daniel Brooks

Role: Featured artist whose painting is being shown at the gallery.

Motive: Victor refused to pay the full amount that was originally promised for the painting.

Alibi: Says he was speaking with several guests near the exhibit area during the time of the murder.

Suspicious Detail: Witnesses say he looked upset earlier in the evening after speaking with Victor.

Notes: Financial disagreement could give him a reason to confront Victor.

Olivia Grant

Event Manager

Click to inspect suspect file

Olivia Grant

Role: Event manager responsible for organizing the exhibition.

Motive: Victor recently blamed her for mistakes in the event budget and threatened to fire her.

Alibi: Claims she was setting up drinks and refreshments for guests in the lobby.

Suspicious Detail: She had access to most areas of the gallery, including the office hallway.

Notes: Losing her job could have been a strong motive.

Marcus Hale

Marcus Hale

Role: Wealthy art collector attending the event to purchase artwork.

Motive: Victor suddenly increased the price of the featured painting during the event.

Alibi: Says he stepped outside to take a phone call during the time of the murder.

Suspicious Detail: No one clearly remembers seeing him leave the building.

Notes: He had a financial reason to be angry with Victor.

Tyler Reed

Tyler Reed

Role: Security guard responsible for monitoring cameras and building security.

Motive: Victor recently discovered Tyler had been entering the storage room without permission.

Alibi: Says he was checking the security system when the cameras went offline.

Suspicious Detail: His keycard was used to enter the hallway near Victor’s office.

Notes: His job gave him full access to the security system.

Sarah Kim

Role: Journalist invited to cover the gallery exhibition.

Motive: Victor refused to give her exclusive access to the gallery’s upcoming collection.

Alibi: Says she was interviewing guests in the main hall.

Suspicious Detail: She was seen walking near the office hallway earlier in the evening.

Notes: She may have been trying to get a story.

Ethan Parker

Role: Assistant curator who helps manage the gallery’s artwork.
