Welcome to our Murder Mystery!

A murder with no answers. Can you solve it?

During a private exhibition at the Langley Art Gallery, owner Victor Langley was found dead in his office late at night. The event was invitation only, and only a small group of guests and staff were inside the building. Shortly before the murder, the security cameras briefly went offline, and witnesses reported hearing an argument coming from Victor’s office. Because the gallery was closed to the public, the killer must be one of the people attending the event. As the detective assigned to the case, you must review the timeline, suspects, and evidence to determine what really happened.
